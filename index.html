Convenient

You provide a button and the contents of your popover and call it the day. It is really easy to get started.

Comes with Themes

k-pop comes with themes. Pick one you like or roll your own. If you don't like styling at all that is fine – just don't set a theme.

Vue Simple Portal + Popper

k-pop is built using Vue, Vue Simple Portal and Popper.js.
